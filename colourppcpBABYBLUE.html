Baby Blue PPCP Granules

Gentle Pastel Blue for Sensitive Applications

Our Baby Blue PPCP Granules offer a soft, pastel blue color that is particularly suitable for products designed for children or healthcare applications. The copolymer formulation provides excellent chemical resistance while maintaining a gentle appearance.

The baby blue pigment is non-toxic and meets stringent safety standards, making it appropriate for toys and medical devices. The material processes easily with excellent flow characteristics.

Key Features:
  • Soft, pastel blue color
  • Non-toxic formulation
  • Excellent chemical resistance
  • Good processability
