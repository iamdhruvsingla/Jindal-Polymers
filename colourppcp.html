Colour PPCP Products

Premium Quality Polypropylene Copolymer Granules in Vibrant Colors

Jindal Polymers' Colour PPCP (Polypropylene Copolymer) granules are engineered for superior performance in a wide range of applications. These granules combine excellent impact strength with vibrant, long-lasting colors, making them ideal for products that require both durability and aesthetic appeal.

Our PPCP range offers enhanced thermal and chemical resistance compared to standard PP, along with better clarity and flexibility. The copolymer structure provides improved stress crack resistance, making these granules perfect for containers, closures, and household products.

We maintain strict quality control to ensure consistent color matching, melt flow characteristics, and mechanical properties across all batches of our Colour PPCP products.
