Milky Colour PP Products

Premium Quality Milky Colored Polypropylene Homopolymer Granules

Jindal Polymers' Milky Colour PP (Polypropylene Homopolymer) granules combine the superior properties of PP with soft, milky pastel colors. These granules offer excellent stiffness, heat resistance, and a distinctive milky appearance that diffuses light beautifully.

Our Milky Colour PP range maintains all the mechanical advantages of standard PP while providing consistent, aesthetically pleasing pastel colors. The milky formulation enhances color depth and provides a unique matte finish that's highly sought after in premium packaging and consumer products.

We maintain strict quality control to ensure color consistency, opacity levels, and mechanical properties across all batches of our Milky Colour PP products.
