Black PP & PPCP Products

Premium Quality Black Polypropylene Homopolymer and Copolymer Granules

Jindal Polymers offers a comprehensive range of Black PP (Polypropylene Homopolymer) and PPCP (Polypropylene Copolymer) granules designed for applications requiring UV protection, weatherability, and aesthetic appeal. Our black compounds provide excellent opacity, consistent coloration, and superior mechanical properties.

Our Black PP products are formulated with premium carbon black and UV stabilizers to ensure long-term performance in outdoor applications. The PPCP variants combine the stiffness of PP with enhanced impact resistance, making them ideal for demanding applications.

We maintain strict quality control measures to guarantee batch-to-batch consistency in terms of color depth, melt flow index, and mechanical properties across our entire Black PP and PPCP product line.

Black BOD Granules

Standard black polypropylene granules with UV stabilization, ideal for outdoor applications. Provides excellent weatherability and good mechanical properties for automotive components, industrial parts, and consumer goods.

Black BOD (Super Extra Z Black) Granules
